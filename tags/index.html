Analysis and Methods for the Simulation of Deformation, Failure and Coupled Processes Across Scales

ASCE EMI 2020

2020 International Conference of the Engineering Mechanics Institute April 5-8 Durham, UK Details for the conference can be found in the conference website. The deadline for submission of abstracts is the 15th of November, 2019. Minisymposium Organizers Matteo Icardi, The University of Nottingham Savvas Triantafyllou, The University of Nottingham Emilio Martínez-Pañeda, Imperial College London MS-52: Analysis and Methods for the Simulation of Deformation, Failure and Coupled Processes Across Scales
NOVEL MODELLING AND NUMERICAL APPROACHES FOR FLOW AND TRANSPORT PROCESSES IN POROUS MEDIA

WCCM ECCOMAS 2020

14th World Congress on Computational Mechanics and 8th European Congress on Computational Methods in Applied Science and Engineering Paris, July 19-24 2020 MS292: NOVEL MODELLING AND NUMERICAL APPROACHES FOR FLOW AND TRANSPORT PROCESSES IN POROUS MEDIA Organisers Marco Berardi, CNR Mario Putti, Padova Matteo Icardi, Nottingham Bagus Muljadi, Nottingham Details about the conference can be found here The deadline for submissions is December 15, 2019. Description Porous media are ubiquitous in many industrial and natural processes, and there is a growing need of understanding and predicting the multiscale flow and transport phenomena in key energy and manufacturing sectors such as subsurface reservoirs, vadose and root zones, composite materials, filtration, and catalytic reactors, just to name a few.

Welcome to our new PhD students

Welcome to our new PhD students Robert and Dylan: Robert Barnett will work on a project related to the multiscale modelling and simulation of charged particles in porous media and lithium-ion batteries, supervised by Matteo Icardi and John King (Mathematics). Adilan Mahdiyasa “Dylan” will work on the modelling and simulation of peatlands and their effect on carbon storage, supervised by David Large, Bagus Muljadi (Engineering), and Matteo Icardi (Mathematics).
