MultiForM

multi-scale fluid dynamics in porous media

Website under construction

MULTIFORM is an interdisciplinary research group at the University of Nottingham, focused on:

  • Mathematical modelling and simulation of complex fluid flow in heterogeneous and porous systems;
  • Open-source low-Re flow and transport simulators, including single and multiphase systems, heat and mass transfer, particle transport, complex geometries;
  • Multiscale analysis, coarse-graining, and upscaling.

Other areas of interests are geomechanics, uncertainty quantification, numerical analysis, experimental and imaging techniques, etc…
